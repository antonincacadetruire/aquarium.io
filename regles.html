Welcome to Aquarium.io!

In this game you will see what the life of a fish looks like in the Ocean. Very cool right? Well, let's say that nowadays it's harder than you think.

In this game you'll be put in the fish's shoes. This game's objective is to see what it's really like to survive as a fish.

First step: choose your character and the level (chill/normal/hell) and start the game.
Second step: avoid all of the falling objects that can kill the fish using your mouse.

Your score depends on how long you can stay alive.

This game's objective is to raise awareness of the problems associated with ocean pollution.

Good Luck! And have a good swim !
